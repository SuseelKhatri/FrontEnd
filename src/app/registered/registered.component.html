Registered Members

As per the Nepal Rastra Bank Regulation, all banks and financial institutions that are registered in Nepal Rastra Bank and engaged in credit lending of any amount must be registered in Kendra as its member. Only the registered members can avail the credit reports and other credit information services provided by Kendra.
