Benefits to Members and Others

The Borrowers

Will have faster and easier access to credit and are entitled to reduced cost of borrowing in case of demonstrated credit performance.

For Small and Medium Enterprises

It reduces the high risk perception among lenders, reduces the cost of borrowing, and gives them faster and easier access to credit.

Banks and Financial Institutions

Are benefited in terms of increased market penetration, operating efficiencies, and comprehensive risk review of clients.

For Regulator

Helps reduce non-performing loans and provides a credit risk monitoring mechanism without increasing risk.
